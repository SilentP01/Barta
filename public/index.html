Screenshot & Recording Protection

Your privacy is non-negotiable. Barta implements advanced hardware-level deterrents against unauthorized screen captures and recordings. Therefore, for your ultimate protection, private connections are strictly disabled on standard mobile phone browsers. To chat securely on a mobile device, download and install the official Barta APK, which enforces complete device-level screenshot and screen recording blocking.
